# Open Source Underwater Glider
## Underwater Glider
Underwater gliders are autonomous underwater vehicles used to collect valuable scientific data from water column with minimal effort. Since there is no external moving part, they are extremely efficient and can cruise the ocean for many months at a time.
![alt text](https://github.com/e-abdi/Open-Source-Glider/blob/main/Pics/Glider%20Example.PNG)
## This Project
This project serves as an effort in making underwater gliders more accessible to students and general public and to promote citizen science. 
![alt text](https://github.com/e-abdi/Open-Source-Glider/blob/main/Pics/Current%20design.png)
It was inspired by excellent work of [Alex William](https://hackaday.io/project/20458-osug-open-source-underwater-glider) in his Hackaday project. Most of the mechanical parts are basically copy of his designs, however I took a slighly different approach to buoyancy drive system. I decided to go for pumping oil in and out of an external bladder (similar to most commercial gliders) as oppose to pumping and draining sea water. Also I decided to create a modular system with three Bluerobotics standard 4 inch pressure housing so that it could easiy be upgraded to aluminium later on for a higher depth rating.
